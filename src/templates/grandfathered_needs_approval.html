Grandfathered permits require approval from your leasing office. To get a free grandfathered permit, please follow these steps:

  1. Visit your Leasing Office and let them know you need a grandfathered parking permit.
  2. Provide your vehicle details to the leasing office: make, model, year, color, license plate number, and state.
  3. Have your leasing office email us at support@parkm.com to approve the free permit and provide your vehicle information.

Once we receive approval from your leasing office, we will issue the grandfathered permit to your account right away.
