Thank you for reaching out! Here's how to create your ParkM account:

  1. Visit parkm.app
  2. Search for your community/apartment complex name
  3. Click on your community and follow the prompts to create an account
  4. You will need a valid email address and your vehicle information (license plate, make, model, year, color, and state)

Once your account is created, you can log in anytime at parkm.app/account/login to manage your permits and vehicles.
